D'ye see the scars on me head where I was struck with flying bricks? There ain't a child at the spindles but what would curse me name. Me only friend is the company. It's not me duty, but me bread an' butter an' the life of me children to stand by the mills. That's why.”

“Was Jackson to blame?” I asked.

“He should a-got the damages. He was a good worker an' never made trouble.”

“Then you were not at liberty to tell the whole truth, as you had sworn to do?”
